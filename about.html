A Little About Myself

My name is Cory Wisniewski, and I come from Mesa, Arizona. I started learning to program with computers when I was about 12 years old, where I downloaded the free version of the software Game Maker Studio. From here, I was only playing with the idea of creating something for fun that would actually be fun, and I began to develop small platform games. I then began taking computer programming classes at my high school where I learned the basics of web development and application design. We created projects such as point and click adventure games, pac man games, and personal websites. Since then, I have began taking courses online entailing UX Design and Full Stack Development.
